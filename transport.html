Transport

We will be providing transportation to and from the wedding venue on the day.

There will be two designated collection points in Funchal — one in the Lido area and one in Funchal Centre — with return transfers arranged in the evening.

Further details and timings will be shared closer to the date. All further updates will appear on this page, so please check back nearer the time.

We want to make sure everyone gets there (and back!) safely and comfortably.
