Accommodation

The wedding venue does have rooms available — but space is limited, and we’ll be arranging transport to and from Funchal for all guests.

While Santana is beautifully scenic and wonderfully peaceful, it’s fairly remote and doesn’t offer much to do nearby. For most guests, staying in Funchal is a better option. It’s the island’s vibrant capital, with plenty of restaurants, hotels, bars, and beaches.

If you’re unsure where to book or want a second opinion, just ask Eddie!
