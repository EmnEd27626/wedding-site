FAQs

Can I bring a plus one?
No! That’s why your invite doesn’t say plus one. We wish we could invite everyone’s guests but numbers are tight, and we want to celebrate with our closest friends and family.

Are kids invited?
This again? Read the invite. (We love your little ones, but this will be an adult celebration.)

Is there parking at the venue?
Yes, plenty! But we recommend using our transport options for ease (and more poncha).

Will it be warm?
Generally, yes — but Santana is on the north side of the island and can be chilly at night, so bring a layer!
